SCO – EVERSEN: NEW MIS SCAN / PRODUCT SWITCH SCREENS
KB0035563
====================================================================

📌 OVERVIEW:
--------------
Kroger is rolling out updated **Everseen Mis Scan and Product Switch screens** across self-checkout (SCO) lanes. These new screens are more intuitive and are designed to help reduce shrink by enabling quicker and more accurate responses from SCO associates.

📍 Stores must validate that ALL SCO lanes received the updated screens.
📍 Use validation process below.

📣 NOTE:
---------
- For escalations: Take an **EXTRACT** at the time of the issue.
- Include in the incident:  
  ✅ Date & Time  
  ✅ Terminal/Lane Number  

====================================================================
✅ VALIDATING NEW MIS SCAN SCREENS:
----------------------------------------
1. **Scan two items** (they must be added to the transaction).
2. **Slide a third item over the scanner WITHOUT scanning** it.
3. **Place item into bagging area.**
4. Confirm that the **new Mis Scan screen appears**.
5. **Repeat steps on every SCO lane.**

====================================================================
✅ VALIDATING NEW PRODUCT SWITCH SCREENS:
--------------------------------------------------
**You will need:**  
🟢 *Extra 15 Stick Spearmint Gum (UPC: 0220000899)*

Steps:
1. **Scan two items** (ensure they are added to the transaction).
2. **Cover the barcode of another product** with the Spearmint gum.
3. **Scan the gum (UPC: 0220000899)** so it gets read instead of the original product.
4. **Place item into bagging area.**
5. Confirm the **Product Switch screen appears**.
6. **Repeat on each SCO lane.**

====================================================================
🛠️ IF THE NEW SCREENS DO NOT DISPLAY:
----------------------------------------------------
1. **Reboot the SCO lane.**
2. **Re-check** to see if the new screens now appear.
3. If issue persists:
   - Have store **take an extract**.
   - Escalate to **APP-POS**:
     - **Sev. 3** – if issue is on **one lane**
     - **Sev. 2** – if issue is on **ALL lanes**

====================================================================
📞 QUICK ESCALATION CHECKLIST:
----------------------------------------
- ✅ Extract taken
- ✅ Time & date of issue
- ✅ Lane number
- ✅ Screenshot if possible
- ✅ Note if affecting one lane or all

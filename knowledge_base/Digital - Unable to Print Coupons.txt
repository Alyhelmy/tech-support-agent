Digital - Unable to Print Coupons

KB0011381 


Synopsis
A customer reports they are unable to print their clipped coupons list

 

Solution
Possible problems may include: printing a blank page, mixing the categories, or error messages. Gather all information including:

  - Customer name

  - Customer contact information

  - Email address associated with digital account

  - Web Browser details should also be included when available.

  - Banner site the customer is logged into.

If the customer is using the mobile app, please include:

  - Phone type (i.e. iPhone 5 or Android)

  - OS version

  - App version

Assign the ticket to App-Dig-Web-Offers.
